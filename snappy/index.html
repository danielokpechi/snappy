Awesome Features

Here are some of the awesome features and services Snappi offers to customers and vendors!

Restaurants

Snappi offers a wide selection of restaurants within the Ogun State area for customers to choose from! Each restauant's page has information on their menu, special offers and distance from the customer's location.

Supermarkets

Snappi provides an extensive collection of thriving supermarkets carefully curated for your convenience and satisfaction. Explore a wide range of options and choose from our handpicked selection.

Shop4Me

Snappi's 'Shop4Me' section allows customers to conveniently make an order for items from the local markets or retail stores and have them delivered for a cheap price. It's a time saving, pocket-friendly, stress-reducing solution for shopping.

Medicals

The Snappi app not only provides food services, but also a section for non-intensive medical/health supplies from leading pharmacies.

Pick-n-Drop

Snappi offers a pick and drop (errand) section to meet your package transport needs. Simply access the errand section of the Snappi app to have a rider pick up your package and deliver it to who or where you specify.

Home services

Snappi offers multiple home services like plumbing, cleaning, painting, and more by connecting you with professionals who provide such services within your area.

Secure payment options

Snappi prioritizes your security and offers secure payment options, allowing you to make transactions with confidence and peace of mind.

Dedicated Customer Support

Snappi has a dedicated customer support team ready to assist you with any queries or concerns you may have, ensuring a smooth and satisfactory shopping experience.

Order tracking and notifications

Stay updated with real-time order tracking and notifications, so you can know exactly when your food, products and services will arrive.
